About

Not your typical designer.

After over a decade as a product designer at Sony Music, Vimeo, Angi, and also contracts with Walmart, Intel and Tag1, I discovered something: my real strength was never specialization. It was the ability to jump between disciplines (product, brand, marketing, web) and deliver high-value, high-quality work, fast.

Today I work as an embedded design partner for Tag1 Consulting, a global Drupal agency. Client UX work, internal brand extensions, conference materials, sales support. This is the work I was built for.
